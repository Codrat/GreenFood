Avocado smoothie

Ingredients:

1 avocado
1 banana
150 ml orange juice
100 ml coconut milk
2-3 tbsp oatmeal
3-4 spinach leaves
1 tbsp honey syrup
-quantities for 2 servings-

Preparation:

Blend the oatmeal and the coconut milk. Add the rest of the ingredients and blend again. Pour into glasses and serve. Enjoy!

Carrot salad

Ingredients:

4 cups of carrots
1/4 cup of olive oil
3-4 tbsp fresh lemon juice
1/4 cup of parsley or coriander
1 tbsp cinnamon or caraway
salt, pepper, chilli (optional)

Preparation:

Mix all the ingredients, then cover with foil and leave it in the fridge for 2 hours, up to two days. Served at room temperature or cold.

Detoxification smoothie

Ingredients:

1 banana
1 papaya
2 stevia leaves
500ml water

Preparation:

Blend all ingredients. Serve cold.

Eggplant bruschetta

Ingredients:

2 eggplants
2-3 tomatoes
10-15 basil leaves
4 slices of bread
75-100g feta cheese
2-3 garlic cloves
olive oil
salt and pepper

Preparation:

Grill the eggplants, toast the bread, rub it with basil, garlic and oil, then fill with the other ingredients.

Mushroom bruschetta

Ingredients:

mushrooms
1 tbsp butter
1 tbsp olive oil
bread
parsley

Preparation:

Cut the mushrooms into small pieces and cook them in butter and olive oil. When they are done, add the garlic and let it cook for one more minute. Add ingredients on toasted bread and decorate with parsley.

Tomato bruschetta

Ingredients:

4 small tomatoes (375g)
1 garlic clove
olive oil
5-6 basil leaves
bread
salt and pepper

Preparation:

Cut all the ingredients into small pieces and add on toasted bread. Enjoy!
